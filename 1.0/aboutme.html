About Me

As embarrassing as it is to admit, my desire to enter the digital realm of advertising stems from my addiction to Neopets in middle school. I used my Neopets homepage as an expression of myself back then, and I still use HTML, CSS and Adobe Photoshop (with the addition of several other programs and languages) to express myself today.
