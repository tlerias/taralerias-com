The Project:
We were asked to create a 45 second to 1 minute long animated spot visually conveying ONE of the following themes: Structures, Patterns, Disconnected, OR Chaos. Develop 6-8 words or phrases which represent that theme and use it to guide our animation from scene to scene. The project needed to include a copyright free audio track, therefore I created my own audio track using Garageband.
